Lava

Lava

My very first liquid abstract wallpaper. Designed in Photoshop, using simple raster shapes in selected color palette and liquifying them in different ways so it resembles lava liquids. This wallpaper can be found in Reev Pro.

Metal Lava

Metal Lava

Metal is hard to resemble when it comes to 2D abstract art. Metal Lava follows the same liquid idea of the first one (Lava) but this time I used an image to liquify rather than custom shapes. Making the process much easier and more fun to experiment with. This wallpaper can be found in Reev Pro.

Material 10

Material 10

Google showcased their new material design wallpapers in their latest Pixel phones lineup in 2020. Inspired, I just could not resist to make my own in a similar design but in a different frame and shapes so it looks great on any homescreen. Due to the simplicity of this design, I easily put multiple variations together in Figma. This wallpaper can be found in Reev Pro.

Gradjent Blue

Gradjent Blue

In 2020, I recreated my very first app/theme called Gradjent & served it with 3 brand new variations of this wallpaper.

The idea was to make gradient liquids that defines butter smooth animations of the preset & gradients which the entire preset is based upon. All variations of this wallpaper can be found in Reev Pro.

Blystal Light

Blystal Light

An experimental design created in Photoshop with the mixer brush tool. "Blystal" is a combination of "Crystal" and "Blue". I know I'm a true genius. This wallpaper can be found in Reev Pro with another variation in inverted colors.
