About Me

I’m Grabster, A 21 year old digital artist, content creator and tech enthusiast from Pakistan. I invest most of my time making art and watching YouTube videos. The latter may be YouTube algorithm fault suggesting me memes that I can’t resist watching.

I've been making YouTube videos since 2015 and have built an amazing community of over 16,000 people. My goal is to teach new skills with easy to follow instructions and entertaining commentary.

For the past couple years I have been interested in Android customization and theming in general. I have designed a bunch of presets for Kustom Live Wallpaper Maker (also known as KLWP) and I also made my own Icon Pack called Reev Pro that I update with new content on a weekly basis.

When I’m not working, I listen to music and play games. My music taste is quite diverse but the genres I’m most interested in are Dubstep and Synthwave. My gaming taste however is very specific. I only play fast paced games that keeps me on the edge such as first person shooters, racing and old-school Contra games that I’ve been trying to beat a speed-run record of.

I’m mostly active on Twitter and Discord. I do exist on other social media sites but I rarely use them. If you want to find me, my username is always @GrabsterTV across all platforms.
